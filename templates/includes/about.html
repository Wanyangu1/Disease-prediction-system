How the system works

The system analyzes input symptoms using machine learning algorithms, correlating patterns with known diseases. It leverages historical health data, identifies relevant features, and predicts the most probable disease. The patients can then book an appointment with an expert in the field of need for further consultation

Authentication

We use secure authentication methods to ensure security of sensitive health data, ensuring patient privacy, maintaining data integrity, and adhering to legal and ethical standards, fostering trust among users.

UI/UX

The system entails an intuitive website where users can view interact with different functions while enjoying efficient accesibility and usability

Backend

The frontend collects user-input symptoms, sending them to the backend via Flask, a web framework. The backend processes the data, predicts diseases using a ML model, and relays the feedback to the frontend for user presentation.

Ophthalmology Department (Eye Care):

Specializes in eye examinations, vision correction, and treatment of eye conditions.

Services: Comprehensive eye examinations, vision correction (glasses, contact lenses), treatment of eye diseases (glaucoma, cataracts), and surgical interventions (cataract surgery, LASIK).

Dermatology Department (Skin Care)

Deals with skin-related issues, including diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions.

Services: Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions (acne, eczema, psoriasis), cosmetic dermatology, skin cancer screenings, and surgical procedures (biopsy, excision)

Gynecology Department (Women's Health)

Specializes in women's reproductive health, offering services such as routine exams and family planning.

Services: Women's wellness exams, prenatal care, family planning, gynecological surgeries, treatment of reproductive health issues (endometriosis, polycystic ovary syndrome), and menopausal care.

Cardiology Department (Heart Health)

Focuses on the diagnosis and management of heart-related conditions and cardiovascular health.

Services: Diagnostic tests (ECG, echocardiogram), treatment of cardiovascular conditions (hypertension, heart disease), cardiac rehabilitation, and preventive care through lifestyle interventions.

General medicine department

Your Comprehensive Health Care Solution Center

Services: Primary care, routine health check-ups, management of common illnesses (colds, flu), preventive care, and referrals to specialized departments for further evaluation and treatment. The General Medicine Department serves as the first point of contact for various health concerns, offering comprehensive and holistic healthcare services.
